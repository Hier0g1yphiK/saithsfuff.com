# Product Overview

saithsfuff.com is a personal website for the content creator and Twitch streamer "saithsfuff." It serves as a central hub for:

- A homepage showcasing an embedded Twitch stream, Instagram feed, and TikTok feed
- A links page (link-in-bio style) with social media profiles and partner/sponsor discount codes
- A portfolio page
- A media kit page for brand collaboration inquiries

The brand aesthetic is whimsical, pastel, and playful — soft pinks, lavenders, and mints with sparkle/star decorations and rounded card elements. The site supports light and dark themes.

Target audience: fans, followers, and potential brand partners looking to connect with saithsfuff across platforms.
